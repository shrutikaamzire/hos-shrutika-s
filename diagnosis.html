Our Diagnostic Services

Blood Test

Comprehensive blood analysis for detecting various health conditions.

X-Ray Imaging

Non-invasive imaging technique to view the internal structure of bones and organs.

Ultrasound

Uses sound waves to produce images of organs and tissues inside the body.

CT Scan

Detailed imaging technique that provides cross-sectional views of the body.

MRI Scan

Uses strong magnets and radio waves to create detailed images of internal body structures.

ECG (Electrocardiogram)

Monitors the electrical activity of the heart to detect abnormalities.
